I want to start by sharing something that made my day today. You see, I grew up in Guadalajara, Mexico, and I always loved it there. It has been a while since I left. I lived in a few places ever since, but I keep tabs with my home town quite often, after all, most of my family and lots of great friends still live there. I come back and visit as often as I can but definitely not often enough.
Rather than me telling you what I love about my city, let me share with you the video that I found today which totally brightened my day. This is one of the most iconinc songs in Mexican music. I bet you've hear it before. I obviously have but never like this. I loved the performances, but what moved me the most are those beautiful images that make my memory travel from the place I call home now to the place I called home growing up.
That's what makes travel special. Travelling not only about seeing new places but is about creating new memories. Those that you will bring with you to your next adventure. Those that make part of you and shaped you at least just a little bit.
